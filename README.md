# Who-is-who-Tool
The app enables users to visually map organizational structures by capturing key information such as names, titles, departments, and responsibilities. Its primary objective is to enhance effective communication among teams within the organisation by creating printable charts.
![How to draw your own institution’s IRO hierarchy easily:
-you can add people by filling in the fields on the upper right corner and click 'Add person' or press Enter

-fill in the name of your organization on the upper left corner

-individual cards can be edited, copied and removed

-make use of the management levels by dragging and dropping people to the right level

-you can add empty cards to use spaces to group teams, departments and supervisors so it reflects your organization

-save your work by clicking 'save' on the downright corner

-load your previously saved version from the 'load' button

-you can print your organizational chart from the 'print' button: note that you can set the chart to 'landscape' layout, and
it’s also possible to modify the scale by going into the 'more settings' option. This will result in a better-proportioned
printed version of the file. Note that you can also save as a pdf file.](https://studioeunice.github.io/Peer-IR-View-Tool/media/help.png)
